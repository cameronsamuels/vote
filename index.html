  • Voting by mail

  • You can vote by mail in Texas if you are 65 or older, have a physical disability, are outside the county during early voting and on election day, or confined in jail. Otherwise, you must vote in person. Your state may require you to fit in a certain criteria to be eligible to vote by mail.
  • Request your ballot by mail The deadline in Texas is October 23. Visit the Secretary of State website to request an absentee ballot by mail.
