  • Voter Registration

  • The deadline in Texas is October 5. Visit the Secretary of State website to register in person or by mail.
  • You may have been removed from voter rolls for many reasons: change of address, change of name, and even accidentally during voter purges. Verify whether you are registered at the Secretary of State website

  • How will you vote?

  • You can vote by mail if you are aged 65+, have a physical disability, outside the county during early voting and on election day, or confined in jail. Otherwise, you must vote in person.

  • Voting by mail

  • Request your ballot by mail Visit the Secretary of State website to request a ballot by mail by October 23.

  • Lookup polling information

  • You may vote at any polling location in your county during early voting, but you may have to vote at a specific location on election day. Visit the Secretary of State website to find your polling location(s).
  • Polls are open from 7am to 7pm on election day, but may have different hours during early voting. Visit the Secretary of State website to find the hours for your polling location(s).
